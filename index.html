§ IX

Node Discovery & Scalability

auto-join · capacity 32 · v5.6
The sink maintains a dynamic registry of nodes. Any node — including ones unknown at compile time — will automatically join the mesh upon its first packet (or NODE_HELLO broadcast at boot). Nodes inactive for 3 minutes become stale and are removed after 10 minutes of silence. This allows up to 32 nodes to be added or removed without reconfiguring the sink.
